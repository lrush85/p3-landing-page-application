THE QUEST STORY

Quest Coffee was established in 2002 by the Herman family of Lynden, Washington. With a dream of starting a chain of coffee shops, the Hermans pooled their talents and resources to create the first Woods Coffee shop in Lyden

OUR ROASTERY

Opened in spring of 2016, the Roastery is the all-new way to experience coffee in Whitcom County

Visit Quest Coffee Roastery to see where all Quest Coffee is roasted fresh, and make sure to grab a bag of our whole bean coffee, or a growler of our Cold Brew, all produced on site.
